Digital Safety for Students

Digital safety means being careful and responsible while using the internet, social media, games, and online apps.

Students spend many hours online, so learning how to stay safe is very important.

Why It Matters

  • Protects personal information
  • Prevents cyberbullying
  • Keeps students safe online
