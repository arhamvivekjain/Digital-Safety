Common Online Risks

Cyberbullying

Cyberbullying happens when someone is hurt or bullied through messages, comments, or posts online.

Online Scams

Fake links or messages can trick people into sharing personal information.

Privacy Problems

Sharing too much online can allow strangers to see private details.
